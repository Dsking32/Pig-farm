Our Breed

We raise the Large White — Nigeria's finest pig breed

The Large White (Yorkshire) is one of the world's most respected pig breeds, prized for its feed efficiency, calm temperament, and strong breeding performance. At GreenPasture Farm, we supply both quality pork and live breeding stock for farmers and restaurants across Nigeria.

🍖

Exceptional Meat Quality

Large Whites produce excellent meat and reliable breeding stock, making them ideal for live sales, farm expansion, and premium pork production.

🌱

Superior Feed Efficiency

Reaches market weight faster with less feed, making it the most economical breed for Nigerian pig farming. Our pigs hit 90–100 kg in under 6 months.

🐣

High Litter Size

Our Large White sows consistently produce 10–14 piglets per litter — one of the highest among all commercial breeds — making them ideal for growing your own farm.

🌤️

Climate-Adapted Stock

Our bloodlines have been carefully selected for resilience in Nigeria's tropical heat, ensuring healthy growth and low mortality all year round.

Know Your Pig

Every cut, explained

From snout to tail, every part of our Large White pigs is useful. Here's a guide to the cuts and live stock options we offer, whether you need fresh meat or healthy breeding stock.

Cuts diagram

Shoulder / Boston Butt

Rich, well-marbled cut from the upper front leg. Incredibly flavourful when slow-cooked or smoked for hours.

Stewing BBQ Suya

Pork Loin

The prized lean cut from the back. Mild flavour, low fat, and incredibly tender. Great for chops or roasting whole.

Roasting Chops Grilling

Pork Belly

Our most popular cut. Fatty, juicy layers that render down beautifully in the pan or over an open fire.

Frying Grilling Asun-style

Spare Ribs

Meaty bones from the belly side, perfect for Nigerian pepper soup, outdoor BBQ, or slow braising in sauce.

Pepper Soup BBQ Braising

Ham (Hind Leg)

Large, lean rear leg cut. Ideal for whole roasting at events, slicing for suya, or curing and smoking.

Events Roasting Smoking

Head & Offal

Ears, snout, trotters, liver, heart, and intestines. Beloved for Nigerian soups, pepper soup, and point-and-kill spots.

Pepper Soup Offal Fry Soup
